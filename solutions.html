DroneLogbook Energy Solutions for Industrial Drones

Operating drones in the energy sector requires a tool for comprehensive operations tracking. DroneLogbook.energy Simplifies administrative tasks by providing the tool you need to manage your operations in an easy to use platform, so you can focus on flying.

DroneLogbook Energy Private Label

Our Private Label platform provides an added level of security, operational flexibility and customization to those users who have elevated security and operational needs. This platform was developed with the idea that large organizations have the need for centralized monitoring for compliance/operation management while allowing for distributed local operations. A few key components include:

  • Master Admin for Central Oversight
  • Multiple Organizations (to allow for segregated data)
  • Ability to Transfer Assets/Pilots between organizations
  • Master Personnel/Flight/Equipment Overview
  • Event Triggers (Flights exceeding X altitude etc..)
